# SipUp Daily – App Support

SipUp Daily is an iOS app designed to help you track your daily water intake, build healthy hydration habits, and receive reminders to drink water throughout the day.

The app is simple, intuitive, and works entirely offline.

## Privacy

SipUp Daily does not require user accounts and does not collect personal information directly.

The app stores hydration data locally on your device using on-device storage only.

SipUp Daily uses **Google AdMob** to display ads. AdMob may collect certain data automatically, such as device identifiers, approximate location, and usage data, for advertising and analytics purposes. This data is not linked to the user’s identity by SipUp Daily.

For more information, please refer to Google’s privacy policy:  
https://policies.google.com/privacy
